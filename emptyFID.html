You need to set a valid FID for the e-Form you want to open, which is usually a three-digit number.

1) Get the FID for the e-Form you want to open. Alternatively, just copy the entire URL.


2) Open the Settings page.


2) Then paste the FID/URL into the "eForm ID" field for the associated button.


3) Click the 'Save' button.

4) If the E-Chart is currently open, refresh the page.
